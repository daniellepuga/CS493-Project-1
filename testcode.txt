CURL TEST COMMANDS:

Business Tests:

User may fetch detailed information about a business:
curl -v http://localhost:3000/businesses/2

User may get a list of businesses:
curl -v http://localhost:3000/businesses

Business owners can add their business to the application:
curl -v POST -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"businessName": "Test123", "businessAddress": "123 Cherry Tree Lane", "businessCity": "Bend", "businessState": "Oregon", "businessZip": "55555", "businessPhone": "555-123-4567", "businessCategory": "Restaurant", "businessSubcategory": "Pizza", "businessWebsite": "", "businessEmail": ""}' http://localhost:3000/businesses

Business owners may modify any information listed above for an already existing business:
curl -v -X PUT -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"businessName": "Test123", "businessAddress": "456 New Address Lane", "businessCity": "WeMoved", "businessState": "Oregon", "businessZip": "55555", "businessPhone": "555-123-4567", "businessCategory": "Restaurant", "businessSubcategory": "Pizza", "businessWebsite": "", "businessEmail": ""}' http://localhost:3000/businesses/3

Business owners may remove a business listing from the application:
curl -v -X DELETE http://localhost:3000/businesses/3

Review Tests:

Users may write a review of an existing business:
curl -v POST -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"starRating": "3", "costRating": "1"}' http://localhost:3000/reviews

Check that the range verification when posting a new review:
Range is 0 -> 5 for starRating and 1 -> 4 for costRating.
curl -v POST -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"starRating": "7", "costRating": "0"}' http://localhost:3000/reviews
^ This should not post since it does not meet our specifications.

Users may modify a review:
curl -v -X PUT -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"starRating": "5", "costRating": "2"}' http://localhost:3000/reviews/1

Users may delete a review:
curl -v -X DELETE http://localhost:3000/reviews/2
